Stockly

Smart inventory management for growing businesses.

What it does

  • Predicts cash-flow problems before they happen.
  • Tracks inventory, sales, and customer relationships in one place.
  • Turns everyday business data into clear, confident decisions.

Key features

  • Live dashboard — total products, low-stock alerts, daily sales, and active orders.
  • Inventory management.
  • Finances with profit, expenses, tax estimates, and future projections.
  • Calendar & planning with delivery scheduling and SMS / push reminders.
  • One-click reports exported to CSV, TXT, or PDF.
  • Full stock-history audit trail of every movement.
